We have compiled a list of FAQs. Read through before contacting a SuperMod with questions.

How do I enter for a debate?

Go to the dabate post, under the debaters section, click "apply to join supporting team" or "apply to join opposing team". You would be added to a pool of intending debaters.

It lies on the moderator to confirm debaters for the debate. You would be notified via email when confirmed.

How do I become a moderator?

Simply by creating a debate, you become that debate moderator. A supermod may add extra moderators for coordination.

How many moderators, debaters and judges can a debate have?

Debaters in each team are currently limited to 5. That makes 10 in total. Moderators are usually two or one

Can I make a debate closed to outside comments except from debaters and moderators?

Debates are automatically closed to comments from the audience during the debating period. By default, it becomes open to comments when voting starts. However, a moderator can disable comments for the moderator dashboard.

Can I delete or edit my comments?

Yes, comments can be removed by the owner or moderator. Deleting means "removal from comment feed", and may still have its replies shown.

Can I add images to comments?

Images can be added using markdown syntax. Upload your image to an image sharing site like Imgur and link to the url. or post your comment and edit to bring up the markdown editing interface. where you can upload images.

Emoticons can be added via the edit interface.

What is the difference between open and closed debate?

Open debates allow comments from all audience. It is good practice to choose a side using our emoticon when posting comments.

Comments are open to only debaters, moderators and judges in closed debate until after debate ends. A moderator might even enable greater stricness by disabling comments from the audience.

What is Markdown and Mathjax.

Mathdown and MathJax enable good presentation of text. Our editor comes with a preview which allow you to style your comments and post. MathJax helps to prettify maths equations and symbols, making it readable.

Why is the comment box disabled?

Comment box can be disabled due to many reasons which include;

  • You are not logged in.
  • Debate has started and you are not a participant.
  • Debate has ended and moderator has disabled comments from followers
  • Debate has not started.
