We have compiled a list of FAQs. Read through before contacting a SuperMod with questions.

Can I delete or edit my comments?

Yes, comments can be removed by the owner or moderator. Deleting means "removal from comment feed", and may still have its replies shown.

Can I add images to comments?

Images can be added using markdown syntax. Upload your image to an image sharing site like Imgur and link to the url.

Emoticons are also supported.

What is Markdown and Mathjax.

Mathdown and MathJax enable good presentation of text. Our editor comes with a preview which allow you to style your comments and post. MathJax helps to prettify maths equations and symbols, making it readable.

Why is the comment box disabled?

Comment box may be disabled if you are not logged in or talk is closed for comments.
